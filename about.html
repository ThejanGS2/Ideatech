Our Story

Innovation from Day One

Ideatech (PVT) LTD began with a simple idea: that technology should be a catalyst for growth, not a barrier. Founded by a team of passionate engineers and creatives, we started as a small software house and have grown into a multi-faceted digital solutions provider.

From day one, our focus has been on quality and integrity. Whether we're building a simple website for a startup or a complex super-app like Roam Ceylon, we bring the same level of dedication and precision to every line of code.
